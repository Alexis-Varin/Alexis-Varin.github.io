About

I am a research engineer who focuses mainly on the analysis of transcriptomic data, such as bulk RNA-seq and microarray, and specialize in single-cell RNA-Seq, where I have developed an expertise in a large array of methods: alignment of sequenced data, Seurat analysis (QC, integration, etc) and more specialized analysis such as pseudotime inference, cell-cell communication, variant calling, and much more...
